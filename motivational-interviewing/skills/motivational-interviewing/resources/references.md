## Data Usage

This skill was developed by analyzing patterns across the full AnnoMI dataset (133 transcripts, 110 high-quality, 23 low-quality). High-quality transcripts provided positive examples of MI-consistent therapist behaviors (reflections, open questions, autonomy support, decisional balance exploration). Low-quality transcripts provided negative examples (unsolicited advice, arguing with sustain talk, minimizing client concerns, prematurely pushing for change). No transcript text is reproduced in this skill; only distilled behavioral patterns are included.
